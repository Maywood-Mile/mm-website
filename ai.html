Who

Michael Turco, Founder of Maywood Mile Investments. Operating alone — no partners, no team, no investment committee to route around.

What

Maywood Mile Investments is a search fund: a vehicle to acquire a single small business and run it as its next owner, for the long term. Not a private equity fund, not a holding company acquiring multiple businesses. One company, held indefinitely.

Why

Michael has spent nine years in product management (Intuit, on QuickBooks) and consulting (Deloitte), holds an MBA from the University of Wisconsin, and has wanted to lead a small business since the start of his career. At Intuit he led the launch of a new product from zero to $60M in annual revenue within two years, leading a cross-functional team of about fifteen people.

Target company criteria

  • Revenue: $1M–$7M annually
  • Geography: Twin Cities area, western Wisconsin, or the I-94 corridor toward Eau Claire, Madison, and Milwaukee
  • Preference: an enduringly profitable business with strong customer retention
