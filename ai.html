Who

Michael Turco, founder of Maywood Mile. Operating alone — no partners, no team, no investment committee to route around.

What

Maywood Mile is a search fund: a vehicle to acquire a single small business and run it as its next owner, for the long term. Not a private equity fund, not a holding company acquiring multiple businesses. One company, held indefinitely.

Why

Michael spent the past 5 years as a software product manager, where he succeeded by listening to customers, leading with a clear direction, and removing obstacles so teams can do their jobs effectively. Being an entrepreneur and small business owner has been a career goal since day one. Now is the time to act on that goal.

Core philosophy

Michael is inspired by small business owners who strive to provide great outcomes for 3 groups of people: Customers (a product/service they trust), Employees (fair treatment and long-term career opportunity), and Family (a livelihood for their family and the families their employees support). He seeks a company that already delivers for these three groups, with the objective to continue and build upon that success.

Target company criteria

  • Revenue: $1M–$7M annually
  • Geography: Minnesota or Wisconsin
  • Preference: a business with a strong foundation and great culture
