My Approach

I am inspired by small business owners who strive to provide great outcomes for 3 groups of people:

  • Customers: Provide a product/service that customers trust.
  • Employees: Provide a work place where people are treated fairly and can build a long-term career.
  • Family: Provide a livelihood for their family and protecting the livelihoods of the families their employees support.

I am seeking a company that already delivers for these three groups, and my objective is to continue and build upon that record of success. The ideal company has annual revenue between $1 million and $7 million and calls Minnesota or Wisconsin home.

If you are thinking about retiring or considering selling your business, I'd love to connect.
