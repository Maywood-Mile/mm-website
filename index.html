Welcome

I'm Michael Turco, and I'm looking for one good business to own and run, for the long haul. After nine years building products and leading teams, I'm searching for a single small business to buy and lead as its next owner — not to flip it.

The name comes from a mile-long loop by my family's lake cottage in northern Wisconsin, one we've raced every summer for years. The loop starts where it ends. That's the idea: I'm buying to hold, not to flip.

If you're thinking about what comes next for the business you built, I want to talk about it. You can read more about how I approach this, or reach me directly.
